About The Event

Run For Health, Community & A Clearer Vision

Lace up your running shoes and join us for the SmartVision Amaravathi 10K Run 2026 in the heart of Vijayawada! Sponsored by SmartVision Eye Hospitals, this premier fitness event brings together thousands of runners, fitness enthusiasts, and families to promote healthy vision and an active lifestyle.

Whether you are a seasoned competitive athlete chasing a personal best or a first-time runner looking to enjoy a fun morning with friends, there is a place for you on our track. Let's run together for health, community, and a clearer vision for the future!
