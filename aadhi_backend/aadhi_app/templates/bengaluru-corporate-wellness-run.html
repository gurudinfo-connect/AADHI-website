Bengaluru Corporate Wellness Run 2026

Run for a Healthy Life

Join us for the Bengaluru Corporate Wellness Run 2026, a premier fitness event organized by AADHI Events & Entertainment to inspire healthier workplaces and active lifestyles.

Taking place on Sunday, 15 November 2026 at NICE Road, Bengaluru, this event brings together corporate employees, business leaders, fitness enthusiasts and families for a memorable race-day experience.
