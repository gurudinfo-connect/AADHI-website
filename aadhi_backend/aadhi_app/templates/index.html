About Aadhi Events & Entertainments

Aadhi Events & Entertainments is a leading event management company dedicated to creating memorable experiences through professionally organized sporting events, corporate wellness programs, marathons, entertainment shows, and community celebrations.

With a passion for fitness and excellence, we bring together runners, families, corporates, fitness communities, and sponsors on one platform to inspire healthier lifestyles and stronger communities.

Every event is planned with international standards, ensuring participant safety, seamless organization, engaging entertainment, and unforgettable memories.
