Telangana Half Marathon 2K26

Run for Honor, Run for Pride

📍 Gachibowli Stadium, Hyderabad 🏃 5K · 10K · 21K Half Marathon 📅 Sunday, 25 January 2026

Event Overview

Hyderabad's Premier Half Marathon Experience

The Telangana Half Marathon 2K26 successfully concluded on Sunday, 25th January 2026 at the iconic Gachibowli Stadium, Hyderabad. Organized by Global Events & Entertainment in partnership with the Youth 4 Jobs Foundation, this massive community run served as a powerful Run for Disability Awareness and a Gratitude Run for the Indian Army.

Bringing together over 1,500 passionate runners, professional athletes, and fitness enthusiasts, the marathon celebrated the spirit of unity, endurance, and deep-rooted respect for our heroes.

The Telangana Half Marathon 2K26 stood out as one of Hyderabad's most meaningful fitness events of the year — a Run for Disability Awareness and a Gratitude Run for the Indian Army that brought over 1,500 runners together at Gachibowli Stadium.
